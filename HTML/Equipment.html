Brewing Equipment

These tools help you brew coffee at home in a clean and simple way.

Quick Usage Tips

  • Tip 1: Use fresh beans and clean water
  • Tip 2: Grind size matters (fine for espresso, medium for filter, coarse for french press)
  • Tip 3: Warm your cup and tools first for better taste
  • Tip 4: Keep your equipment clean after every use
